Come and try GAA day in Enfield

The Enfield "Come and Try" Day took place in Donkey Lane last Sunday. A great turnout of 30+ kids were there to be put through their paces. The day ended with the now annual "Adults v Children" match. Despite the Donegal style training regime and some heroic efforts from the ladies in the adults team it was the youths again who won with 2 late goals. Thanks to everyone who helped and attended on the day.

Pat Spillane meets the Shamrocks Youth

On the 2nd of March we had Pat Spillane down do give some training to the youth, a great day was had by all as Pat gave us some great tips, including his famous thoughts on the fist pass!!!
