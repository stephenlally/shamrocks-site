Club Notes 15th May 2014

North London Shamrocks created club history on Saturday in Ruslip as they won their first piece of silverware, the Murphy Cup beating Tara 1.09 - 1.06.

Having agonisingly lost three finals in the past, the squad galvanised by the new management team of Paddy Madigan, Declan Treanor and Brian Conaghon finally got over the final hurdle to land the trophy in the clubs tenth season.

The club know that they need to use this as platform to build on to challenge for the Intermediate Championship, a grade which is very competitive.

North London Shamrocks are proud to announce a competition we are running in conjunction with Castlerea Golf Club. First prize is a package to this year's RYDER CUP including all 4 days of the golfing action, accommodation and £1000 spending money. Second prize is £500. Tickets are only £8 with the draw taking place on Sunday 27th July at Castlerea Golf Club. For more information on this great opportunity please contact the club via the details at the bottom.

Club Notes 15th July 2013

The most eagerly awaited fixture of the opening round of the Intermediate Championship saw NLS in action v Cuchullians in Ruislip last Sunday. Despite a courageous performance from NLS we were beaten by a score of 3-08 to 0-11. After going in two points down at HT, with the breeze at their backs NLS fought back strongly to take a two point lead into the final minutes of the game. However, with only 5 minutes (+ injury time) left, CuChullians stole in for two quickfire goals and tagged on a free and a score from play to take victory. Overall, the scoreline flattered the victors and NLS management were proud of the team and individual players efforts. It's still all to play for with a must win fixture vs Harlseden Harps in a fortnight. Best for NLS on the day were D O'Regan and V Murphy.

Non-playing membership forms are now available from the committee. For £10 you will be entered into a draw for four All-Ireland tickets (2 Hurling, 2 Football). Players and management of NLS are not eligible to enter. This draw is aimed at family, friends and supporters. The club would like to thank Tom Galvin for kindly donating these tickets to the draw. For more information on this please contact any committee member or drop us an email at the address below.
